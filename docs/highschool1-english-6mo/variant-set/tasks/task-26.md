## Questions

### Q1: 어법 네모 (2점)
- **발문**: `(A), (B), (C)의 각 네모 안에서 어법에 맞는 표현으로 가장 적절한 것은?`
- (A) "developed" — simple past for habitual development in childhood ✓; "was developing" implies interrupted action ✗
- (B) "which" — non-restrictive relative clause (comma + clause) requires "which" ✓; "that" cannot follow comma in non-restrictive ✗
- (C) "continues" — subject "his work" is singular active (inspires people) ✓; "is continued" passive doesn't work semantically ✗
- **Correct**: ① developed / which / continues
- **Choice table**:
  ```
       (A)              (B)      (C)
  ①  developed        which    continues   ← CORRECT
  ②  developed        which    is continued
  ③  developed        that     continues
  ④  was developing   which    continues
  ⑤  was developing   that     is continued
  ```

### Q2: 내용 불일치 (2점) — all options paraphrased
- **발문**: `윗글의 내용과 일치하지 않는 것은?`
- **Correct (false statement)**: ② (Yale graduation THEN expedition — "After graduating from Yale, he joined his first expedition" → the expedition was AFTER graduation, not before)
- **Choices** (all paraphrased):
  - ① Grinnell spent his formative years in the vicinity of a park that bears the name of a famous ornithologist. (TRUE: Audubon Park — Audubon = famous bird artist/naturalist)

  - ③ His written contributions to a natural history journal advocated for the protection of wild animals. (TRUE: Forest and Stream articles calling for wildlife conservation)
  - ④ He was involved in establishing a national park that was officially created in the early twentieth century. (TRUE: Glacier National Park in 1910)
  - ⑤ His influence on conservation policy did not end with his passing in the late 1930s. (TRUE: "Even after his death in 1938, his work continues to inspire")
