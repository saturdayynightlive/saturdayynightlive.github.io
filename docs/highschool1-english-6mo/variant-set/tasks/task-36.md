# Task: Passage 36 — 빙하기와 농업 (글 순서)

## Passage Text (exact, with opening paragraph and (A)(B)(C) blocks)

Opening paragraph (in box):
```
During the Ice Age, not only was the planet much colder, with ice sheets covering much of what we call the northern hemisphere today, but, crucially, it was much drier.
```

(A):
```
In this type of climate, farming isn't an option: it's too risky to depend on any one piece of land to produce the energy you need. As the temperature rose and the ice caps melted, we experienced a sudden explosion of life.
```

(B):
```
In Ireland, we often associate the cold with the wet but if it is really cold, there is far less evaporation, fewer clouds, and less rain. Our world in the Ice Age was cold and dry, meaning it was difficult for plants to grow.
```

(C):
```
The world got warmer and wetter, and people started to live around places where they could make food grow most intensively. This didn't happen overnight; it probably took thousands of years, with hunter-gatherers foraging and hunting, while doing a little side hustle in farming.
```
* evaporation: 증발  ** forage: 채집하다  *** side hustle: 부업

Final decision: ② (B)-(A)-(C)

## Questions

### Q1: 글 순서 배열 (2점)
- **발문**: `주어진 글 다음에 이어질 글의 순서로 가장 적절한 것은?`
- **Correct**: ② (B)-(A)-(C)
- **Logic**: Opening introduces the Ice Age as cold and dry. (B) explains why extreme cold meant less evaporation, fewer clouds, and less rain, so it directly elaborates on the opening. (A)'s "In this type of climate" then refers back to that cold/dry climate and explains why farming was not viable before the warming transition. (C) follows the transition in (A), explaining that the world became warmer and wetter and that farming developed gradually.

- **Choice options**:
  - ① (A) - (C) - (B)
  - ② (B) - (A) - (C)  ← CORRECT
  - ③ (B) - (C) - (A)
  - ④ (C) - (A) - (B)
  - ⑤ (C) - (B) - (A)

### Q2: 인과 추론 (2점)
Do not blank "evaporation" because the footnote defines it. Keep the passage sentence intact and ask for the causal logic of block (B).
- **발문**: `(B) 단락의 논리 전개로 가장 적절한 것은?`
- **Correct**: ①
- **Choices**:
  - ① Very low temperatures weaken the movement of moisture into the air, reducing cloud formation and rainfall.
  - ② Low temperatures make Ireland unusually wet, so farming becomes possible despite poor soil.
  - ③ A lack of clouds raises temperatures, causing ice sheets to melt and rainfall to increase.
  - ④ Less rain is presented as the result of farming, not as a condition that limits plant growth.
  - ⑤ Cold weather is described as useful because it keeps plants from using too much energy.
- **Why hard**: Students must reconstruct the causal chain "extreme cold → less evaporation → fewer clouds/rain → poor plant growth" rather than simply matching a vocabulary note.

### Q3: 서술형 — 표현 찾기 (2점)
- **발문**: `[서술형] (C) 단락에서 농업이 갑자기 시작된 것이 아님을 나타내는 영어 표현을 찾아 쓰시오.`
- **Answer**: This didn't happen overnight.
- **Grading**: Full sentence = 2점; 핵심 표현 `didn't happen overnight`만 쓰면 1점
