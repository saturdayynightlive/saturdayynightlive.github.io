### Q1: 문장 삽입 [3점]
- **발문**: `글의 흐름으로 보아, 주어진 문장이 들어가기에 가장 적절한 곳을 고르시오. [3점]`

- **Why hard**: ① might seem right (after "trees play a huge role" → give a fact about their role). But ① is followed by the photosynthesis explanation, which continues the "role" topic. ② is after the specific claim about being a "natural regulator" — the new sentence concretely quantifies this, then ③ extends logically.

### Q2: 요약문 (A)(B) (2점)
- **발문**: `다음은 윗글을 요약한 것이다. 빈칸 (A), (B)에 들어갈 말로 가장 적절한 것은?`
- **Summary**: *Trees play a crucial role in (A)_____ carbon dioxide from the atmosphere, but deforestation has (B)_____ this natural process, leading to rising CO2 levels and global warming.*
- **Correct**: (A) removing / (B) disrupted (= ③)
- **Choice table**:
  ```
       (A)            (B)
  ①  producing       accelerated
  ②  producing       restored
  ③  removing        disrupted    ← CORRECT
  ④  removing        reinforced
  ⑤  storing         improved
  ```
- **Why hard**: (A) "producing" is the opposite (trees remove, not produce CO2). (B) "reinforced" would mean deforestation HELPS the process — clearly wrong. (B) "accelerated" with (A) "removing" would mean "deforestation sped up CO2 removal" — wrong.
