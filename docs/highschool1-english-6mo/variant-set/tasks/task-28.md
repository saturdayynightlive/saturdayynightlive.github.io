# Task: Passage 28 — Food Truck Festival (안내문)

## Passage Text / Layout (exact)
```
Food Truck Festival
Come hungry and leave happy! Join our annual food truck festival with over 20 trucks.

When & Where
• August 16, from 11 a.m. to 9 p.m.
• Emton Park

Festival Highlights
• Free sample tastings between 2 p.m. and 3 p.m.
• Live music performances throughout the festival
• Cookie-making classes for children

Notes
• There is no entry fee.
• Parking is available for free.
※ For the full schedule, visit www.emtontruckfest.com.
```

## Questions

### Q1: 내용 불일치 (2점) — harder paraphrase
- **발문**: `윗글의 내용과 일치하지 않는 것은?`
- **Correct (false)**: ③
- **Choices** (all paraphrased):
  - ① This food truck gathering takes place on a yearly basis.  (TRUE: "annual")
  - ② Visitors may enjoy musical entertainment at any point during the festival.  (TRUE: "Live music performances throughout the festival")
  - ③ Free food samples are available to taste for the entire duration of the event.  (FALSE: only 2–3 p.m., not the full event)
  - ④ Attendees do not need to pay anything to enter the festival grounds.  (TRUE: "no entry fee")
  - ⑤ A hands-on activity is provided specifically for younger visitors.  (TRUE: "Cookie-making classes for children")

### Q2: 서술형 — 단답 (2점)
- **발문**: `[서술형] 다음 물음에 답하시오.`
  
  (1) 주차 비용에 대해 안내문이 언급한 내용을 우리말로 쓰시오.
  - **Answer**: 주차는 무료로 이용 가능하다.
  - **Grading**: 무료 주차 내용 포함하면 정답
  
  (2) 무료 시식(free sample tastings)이 제공되는 시간대를 영어로 쓰시오.
  - **Answer**: between 2 p.m. and 3 p.m.  (또는 2 p.m. to 3 p.m.)
