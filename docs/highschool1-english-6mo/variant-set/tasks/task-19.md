# Task: Passage 19 — Daniel & the Train (심경 변화)

## Passage Text (exact, from exam PDF)
```
Daniel was on his way to the train station for a business trip. He was riding a bus when it suddenly stopped. The driver announced, "We have a mechanical problem." Daniel grew nervous about missing his train. Without hesitation, he got off the bus and took a taxi to the station. Still, he kept worrying about not making it in time. When he finally reached the station, he found out the train was delayed by ten minutes. Once he settled into his seat, he let out a long breath of relief.
```

## Questions

### Q1: 빈칸추론 (2점)
- **Blank position**: Replace "nervous" in "Daniel grew ___ about missing his train."
- **발문**: `다음 빈칸에 들어갈 말로 가장 적절한 것은?`
- **Correct answer**: ② apprehensive
- **Choices** (all single adjectives describing anxiety):
  - ① indifferent
  - ② apprehensive  ← CORRECT
  - ③ elated
  - ④ relieved
  - ⑤ triumphant
- **Why hard**: Students must know "apprehensive" means anxious/worried. "relieved" and "elated" are tempting since they appear in the passage context later.

### Q2: 함의추론 (2점)
- **Underlined phrase**: "he let out a long breath of relief"
- **발문**: `밑줄 친 'he let out a long breath of relief'가 윗글에서 의미하는 바로 가장 적절한 것은?`
- **Correct answer**: ③
- **Choices**:
  - ① he was exhausted after running to the station
  - ② he felt proud of his quick decision to take a taxi
  - ③ he felt relaxed after learning the train had not left without him
  - ④ he was disappointed that the train was not on time
  - ⑤ he was relieved because the mechanical problem was fixed
- **Why hard**: ④ is attractive (train delayed = negative), ⑤ is attractive (mentions mechanical problem). Key: the relief is that the train hadn't left, not about the problem being fixed.

### Q3: 서술형 — 해석 (영→한)
- **발문**: `[서술형] 다음 문장을 우리말로 자연스럽게 해석하시오.`
- **Target sentence**: "Without hesitation, he got off the bus and took a taxi to the station."
- **Model answer**: "주저하지 않고, 그는 버스에서 내려 역까지 택시를 탔다."
- **Grading points**: "Without hesitation" = 주저/망설임 없이, "got off the bus" = 버스에서 내렸다, "took a taxi to the station" = 역까지 택시를 탔다
- **Why not leakable**: Requires understanding and translating; cannot be found in Korean anywhere in the passage.
