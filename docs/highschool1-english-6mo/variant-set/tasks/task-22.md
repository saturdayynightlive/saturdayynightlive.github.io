# Task: Passage 22 — 창의성과 폭넓은 경험 (요지)

## Passage Text (exact)
```
Suppose you have been working on the solution to a challenging technological problem for many years. Let's also suppose that you have been paying attention to whatever else you have encountered that might be related to the problem. In that case, chances are that creative solutions to the problem could suddenly pop up in your mind when you are relaxed. Such "eureka moments" (when great insights lead to discovery or invention) often take place at unexpected times. To enhance spontaneous creativity, therefore, one must pay attention to the things that could potentially lead to valuable outcomes. This does not mean that you must narrow down your interest to one specific topic. On the contrary, breadth of experience is the key to creativity. If your experience is limited to a narrow range of topics, then the scope of your imagination will likewise be narrow, which would be harmful to creativity.
* spontaneous: 자연 발생적인
```

## Passage Modifications
1. **Blank** (Q1): Replace "breadth of experience" with `\rule{4cm}{0.4pt}` → "On the contrary, _____ is the key to creativity."
2. **네모 ×3** (Q2): Apply to:
   - (A): "creative solutions to the problem could suddenly \nemo{pop up / popping up} in your mind"
   - (B): "Such "eureka moments" … often take place at \nemo{unexpected / expectedly} times"
   - (C): "the scope of your imagination will \nemo{likewise / likely} be narrow"

## Questions

### Q1: 빈칸추론 (2점)
- **발문**: `다음 빈칸에 들어갈 말로 가장 적절한 것은?`
- **Blank**: "On the contrary, _____ is the key to creativity."
- **Correct**: ④ a wide range of experiences
- **Choices**:
  - ① deep focus on a single subject
  - ② consistent daily practice
  - ③ logical and systematic thinking
  - ④ a wide range of experiences  ← CORRECT
  - ⑤ collaboration with creative partners
- **Why hard**: ① is the exact OPPOSITE of the correct idea (passage explicitly refutes narrowing interest). ⑤ "collaboration" sounds creative but is not mentioned. Must understand "breadth of experience."

### Q2: 어법 네모 (2점)
- **발문**: `(A), (B), (C)의 각 네모 안에서 어법에 맞는 표현으로 가장 적절한 것은?`
- (A) "pop up" (modal can + bare infinitive) → pop up ✓ / popping up ✗
- (B) "unexpected" (adjective modifying times) → unexpected ✓ / expectedly ✗ (adverb can't modify noun)
- (C) "likewise" (adverb = similarly) → likewise ✓ / likely ✗ (different meaning)
- **Correct combination**: pop up / unexpected / likewise
- **Choice table**:
  ```
       (A)          (B)            (C)
  ①  pop up       unexpected     likewise   ← CORRECT
  ②  pop up       unexpected     likely
  ③  pop up       expectedly     likewise
  ④  popping up   unexpected     likewise
  ⑤  popping up   expectedly     likely
  ```
